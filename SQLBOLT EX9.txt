Ques.1: List all movies and their combined sales in millions of dollars.
Query : SELECT title, (domestic_sales + international_sales) / 1000000 AS 	combined_sales_millions
	FROM movies
	JOIN boxoffice
	ON movies.id = boxoffice.movie_id;


Ques.2: List all movies and their ratings in percent.
Query : SELECT title, rating * 10 AS rating_percent
	FROM movies
	JOIN boxoffice
	ON movies.id = boxoffice.movie_id;


Ques.3: List all movies that were released on even number years.
Query : SELECT title, year
	FROM movies
	WHERE year % 2 = 0;
